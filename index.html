Welcome to Adventure Learning Center, Ltd.

Where children develop a lifelong love of learning! We are a full service preschool and childcare center conveniently located in Ballwin MO just 1 mile south of Manchester Road. Our school offers a strong approach to learning as well as full time child care for infants through Kindergarten.
